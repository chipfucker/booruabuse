title: Rule34Post.prototype.comment()
description: booru-abuse Rule34Post method to comment on a post

The `comment()` method of [`Rule34Post`](./) instances posts a comment under the post as the user signed in via [`Rule34.setCredentials()`](../setCredentials).

## Syntax

```ts
comment(body: string)
```

### Parameters

- `body` &mdash; The content of the comment to post.
